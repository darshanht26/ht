Who, Why and how

Shomont curates the most exclusive luxury travel experiences for people who crave unique elevated getaways. We began by crafting a captivating brand identity that captures Shomont's core vision of representing exclusive, high-end properties globally through their extensive personal connections. The logo symbolizes purposeful luxury, blending elegant curves and sharp lines to emote class, and refinement.

In parallel, we meticulously designed and developed an immersive website that complements the brand identity. We employed a rich color palette and bold yet beautiful typography to create a sense of minimalism and premium quality. The visual direction carefully curated for the website exudes an emotion of sophistication and elegance, perfectly aligning with Shomont's positioning in the luxury travel industry.
